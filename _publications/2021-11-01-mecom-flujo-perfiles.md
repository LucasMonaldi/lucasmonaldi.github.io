*English title: Numerical study of the flow around aerodynamic profiles in subsonic, transonic and supersonic regime*

### Project Overview
This comprehensive study evaluates the aerodynamic performance of three different airfoils across various flow regimes using **ANSYS Fluent**. The research focuses on turbulence model validation and the numerical capture of complex fluid phenomena.

**Key Highlights:**
* **Multi-Regime Analysis:** * **Subsonic:** Validation of the **NACA 0012** airfoil.
    * **Transonic:** Analysis of the **RAE 2822** supercritical airfoil, focusing on shock-wave/boundary-layer interaction.
    * **Supersonic:** Study of an **asymmetric diamond airfoil** and its characteristic shock and expansion wave patterns.
* **Turbulence Modeling:** Evaluation of several models ($k-\omega$ SST, Spalart-Allmaras, and Transition SST) to determine their accuracy in predicting lift ($C_L$), drag ($C_D$), and pressure distributions ($C_p$).
* **Boundary Layer Physics:** Detailed comparison of velocity profiles and friction coefficients against experimental AGARD and NASA data.
* **Numerical Strategy:** Implementation of specific meshing techniques (mixed meshes and trailing edge treatment) to optimize results within the constraints of student-edition software.


Conference paper presented at **MECOM 2021**.
