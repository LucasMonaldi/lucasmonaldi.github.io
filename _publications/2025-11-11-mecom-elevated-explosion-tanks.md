*English title: Elevated explosion simulations on fuel storage tanks using OpenFOAM*

### Project Overview
Atmospheric storage tanks are critical and vulnerable assets in the oil industry. This research investigates the interaction between blast waves and cylindrical tanks, specifically focusing on how the **detonation height** influences the resulting overpressure distribution and structural loading.

**Key Highlights:**
* **Height of Burst (HOB) Effects:** Analysis of how the reflection patterns (Regular vs. Mach reflection) change on the tank surface as the explosion source is elevated.
* **Industrial Risk Assessment:** The study addresses the structural vulnerability of tanks to off-site explosions, providing data to improve safety distances and protective barrier designs.
* **Numerical Modeling:** Simulations conducted using **OpenFOAM** with the **rhoCentralFoam** solver, capturing the complex diffraction and reflection phenomena around the curved geometry of the tanks.
* **Process Safety:** Contributes to the field of "Process Safety and Environmental Protection" by offering a validated computational approach to replace or enhance traditional empirical safety models.
