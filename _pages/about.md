# About Me

I am an **Aeronautical Engineer** and PhD Candidate at the National University of Córdoba (UNC) and CONICET, Argentina. My expertise lies in **Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)**, specifically focused on the simulation of high-energy phenomena and explosions.

### Research & Academic Background
Currently in the 4th year of my doctoral studies, my work involves developing high-fidelity simulations to understand complex fluid behaviors. I have published my research in top-tier journals (Q1 and Q2) and I am a frequent speaker at international aeronautical and mechanical engineering conferences.

### Teaching & Consulting
I have been a Teaching Assistant in **Fluid Mechanics** at UNC for over three years. Driven by the challenge of solving real-world engineering problems, I also provide technical consultancy services, bridging the gap between advanced numerical research and industrial applications.
